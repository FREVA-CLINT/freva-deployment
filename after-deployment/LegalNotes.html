Appendix III: Legal notes as flat-pages#

This appendix gives examples of flat pages containing privacy and terms of conditions. Such statements should be added as flat-pages in the django admin-panel. Flat-pages additional notes that are displayed on the web site. Any flat-page url starting with /legal/ will be added as a link to the footer. For example /legal/privacy/ which could be a flat-page for a privacy statement. Below you’ll find examples of a privacy statement and terms of use.
